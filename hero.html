Who Is Simone Biles?

Simone Biles is a 24 year old American gymnast. She has won a total of 19 olympic medals, as well as 25 world championship medals. To this day, she is considerbly one of the highest-ranking gymnasts in the world. Simone was raised in Spring, Texas, by her grandparents with her sister, Adria Biles. Her grandparents adopted her in 2000; she was six years old. Around this time, she became interested in the intense sport of gymnastics.
